## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

- **Style consistency**: Use "premium healthcare editorial photography" as a style anchor
- **Color palette**: Navy (#0f1e41), gold (#c6a96c), cream (#faf7f2), warm whites
- **Lighting**: Always warm, soft, directional. Never harsh or clinical fluorescent
- **Mood**: Aspirational, confident, warm, trustworthy
- **Avoid**: Stock photo look, overly staged poses, cold clinical blue tones
- **Aspect ratios**: Hero images 21:9, service images 4:3, portraits 3:4, gallery 1:1
