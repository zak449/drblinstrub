About Dr. Blinstrub

This isn't an assembly line. It's a relationship.

Dr. Jonathan Blinstrub is a seasoned dental professional with over a decade of experience delivering exceptional patient care in both civilian and military settings. A U.S. Army veteran and former Captain, he combines technical precision with deep empathy.

He specializes in cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, full-arch restorations, advanced surgical extractions, and IV moderate sedation—making him uniquely equipped to handle the most challenging cases with confidence and compassion.

But what sets him apart isn't just his training. It's the way he treats people. No rushed appointments. No judgment. Just a surgeon who has dedicated his career to giving people back what they thought was gone forever.
