About Dr. Blinstrub

A Surgeon’s Precision. A Veteran’s Discipline. A Doctor’s Heart.

Dr. Jonathan Blinstrub is a seasoned dental professional with over a decade of experience delivering exceptional patient care in both civilian and military settings. A U.S. Army veteran and former Captain, Dr. Blinstrub combines technical precision with deep empathy to treat high-profile, medically complex, and anxious patients.

He specializes in cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, full-arch restorations, advanced surgical extractions, and IV moderate sedation — making him uniquely equipped to handle the most challenging cases with confidence and compassion.
