Comfort & Safety

IV Sedation Dentistry

For patients who experience dental anxiety, Dr. Blinstrub offers IV moderate sedation. This allows you to remain relaxed and comfortable throughout your procedure while we take care of everything.

IV sedation is particularly beneficial for:

✓ Patients with dental anxiety or phobia
✓ Complex or lengthy procedures
✓ Full-arch implant placement
✓ Surgical extractions
✓ Medically complex patients
