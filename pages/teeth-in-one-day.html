Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Teeth in One Day procedure take? +
The surgical procedure typically takes several hours depending on complexity. You arrive in the morning and leave the same day with a full set of temporary teeth. The entire process — from consultation to final restoration — spans a few months to allow for complete implant healing.
Is the procedure painful? +
Dr. Blinstrub is an IV sedation specialist, which means you can be comfortably sedated throughout the entire procedure. Most patients report little to no discomfort and are pleasantly surprised by how smooth the experience is.
How long do dental implants last? +
With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The implant posts are designed to permanently fuse with your jawbone. The restoration (the visible teeth) typically lasts 15–25+ years and can be replaced if needed without disturbing the implants.
What if I don’t have enough bone? +
Dr. Blinstrub is trained in advanced techniques including sinus lifts, bone grafting, and zygomatic/pterygoid implant placement. These specialized approaches often allow patients who were previously told they were not candidates to receive implants successfully.
How much does Teeth in One Day cost? +
The cost varies based on individual needs and treatment complexity. We offer flexible financing options including 0% interest plans. The best way to get an accurate estimate is to schedule a free consultation where Dr. Blinstrub can evaluate your specific situation.
