Meet Dr. Blinstrub

You deserve a dentist who sees you—not just your teeth.

Losing your teeth isn't just a medical problem. It changes the way you eat. The way you speak. The way you present yourself to the world. It makes you feel older than you are. It steals moments you can't get back.

“I don't just restore teeth. I give people permission to smile again—fully, openly, without covering their mouths. That moment when a patient looks in the mirror and sees themselves again? That's why I do this.”

Dr. Blinstrub trained at UCSF, served as a Captain in the U.S. Army, and has spent over a decade performing advanced implant surgeries. He's not the kind of dentist who rushes through appointments. He listens. He explains. And he doesn't stop until it's right.
